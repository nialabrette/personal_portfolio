Services

Don't have a website?

If you don't have an online presence, I can create one for you. With a website, you will attract more customers who will in turn see your business as more trustworthy and successful. We'll layout a website from the ground up and make sure you are prepared for long-term maintenance and hosting.

Have a site but it needs a refresh?

If your site was created a while ago and it feels dated, I can bring it up to speed with new designs and functionality. Time is money. If your site is old and slow, potential customers will click off before they ever see your products. I can refactor your site for faster page loading to retain those eyes on the prize.

Want extra bells and whistles?

If you want to make your site extra fancy, I can offer widgets and apps for your page content. In addition to website building, I also offer copy and editing services as a la carte services.
